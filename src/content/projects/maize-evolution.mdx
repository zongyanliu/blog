# Overview

Our latest preprint, “Extensive genome evolution distinguishes maize within a stable tribe of grasses,” presents a comprehensive analysis of 33 genome assemblies from 27 species within the Andropogoneae tribe. 

The study captures a dynamic picture of genome evolution, revealing the complex history of transposable element proliferation, structural variations, and gene duplications that have shaped the maize genome compared to its wild relatives.
