In modern biology, traditional data formats (like raw fasta or csv) fall short when scaling to millions of sequences. I heavily utilize Zarr and Parquet formats to efficiently stream chunked, compressed genomic data directly to GPUs during training, effectively bypassing memory bottlenecks and accelerating I/O bound problems dramatically.
